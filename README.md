# PhonePe Transaction Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

# Project Overview

The PhonePe Transaction Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed in Power BI to analyze digital payment transactions, customer behavior, and overall business performance. The dashboard transforms raw transaction data into meaningful insights using Power Query, Data Modeling, and DAX, enabling stakeholders to monitor KPIs and make data-driven decisions.

The project focuses on tracking transaction performance across different payment statuses, services, customer age groups, and time periods. It provides a comprehensive view of business growth by identifying transaction trends, user engagement, and payment success rates through interactive visualizations and dynamic filters.

# Key Objectives
Analyze overall transaction performance and revenue generation.
Monitor monthly transaction growth and business trends.
Track payment success, pending, and failed transactions.
Understand customer demographics and usage patterns.
Identify top-performing services and high-value users.
Support strategic business decisions through interactive reporting.

# Dashboard Features
Interactive KPI cards displaying business performance.
Monthly transaction trend analysis using line charts.
Payment status filters (Successful, Pending, Failed).
Service-wise transaction value comparison.
Age-segment analysis (Gen Z, Millennials, Gen X, Boomers).
Top 5 users based on transaction value.
Weekday vs Weekend transaction comparison.
Automated business insights highlighting peak transaction dates and highest-performing services.
Dynamic slicers for month and payment status.

# Business Insights
Achieved an overall 96% transaction success rate, indicating a reliable payment ecosystem.
Loan-related services generated the highest transaction value among all service categories.
Transaction volume peaked during specific months, helping identify seasonal business trends.
Millennials and Gen X contributed the largest share of transaction activity.
Weekday transactions significantly outperformed weekend transactions, indicating higher business-day usage.
The dashboard enables management to quickly identify growth opportunities, monitor operational efficiency, and improve customer engagement.
